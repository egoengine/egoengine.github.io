Egocentric Human Videos → High-Fidelity Robot Demonstrations
Paper Code Video

Abstract

EgoEngine translates egocentric human videos into robot-executable demonstrations via fingertip-to-joint retargeting, object-centric reinforcement learning, and visual synthesis. It closes both visual and action gaps between human and robot embodiments, enabling sample-efficient learning of dexterous manipulation.
